Ramblings About Scientific Accuracy in Media

A thought I've had recently relates to how different media is treated different ways when it comes to "scientific accuracy, and how that affects perception. I was recently watching For All Mankind, which is an alternate-reality space/sci-fi show set amidst a modified and increasingly divergent from OTL space program. I found it interesting apects of it get put in a much stricter lens when compared to what some call "science fantasy", aka Star Wars.

Even compared to Star Trek, Star Wars often is seens in a different light, although, rather uniquely, its tie-in media over the decades has worked to attempt to explain many of its inner workings.
